Privacy Policy

App: La Bodega  |  Package: com.labodegave.app  |  Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how La Bodega ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use the La Bodega mobile application ("the App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryDataWhy we collect it
Account Email address, full name, username, password (hashed) Create and authenticate your account
Phone Phone number SMS verification (OTP) to confirm identity
Identity documents Cédula number or RIF number; photo of ID document Voluntary identity verification for the "Verificado" badge; reviewed only by admins
Profile Profile photo, city and state (Venezuela) Display on your public profile and listings
Listings Title, description, price, photos, location (estado/ciudad), condition Publish classifieds listings visible to other users

Device Expo push notification token Send in-app notifications (new messages, listing updates)
Usage Anonymised events (screen views, feature interactions) Understand how the app is used to improve it (PostHog analytics)

We do not collect precise GPS location. Location is limited to the estado and ciudad you enter manually.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features
  • Verify your identity and phone number
  • Display your listings and profile to other users
  • Deliver in-app messages and push notifications
  • Review identity documents for the "Verificado" badge (admin-only access)
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and handle moderation (reports, strikes, suspensions)
  • Analyse aggregate usage patterns to improve the App
  • Process subscription payments (manual confirmation flow)

4. Identity Documents

Submitting a cédula or RIF number and ID photo is optional and only required if you choose to apply for the "Verificado" badge. Documents are stored in a private, access-controlled Supabase Storage bucket and are reviewed only by authorised administrators. They are never shared publicly or with third parties beyond the Supabase infrastructure used to store them.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active. Deleted when you request account deletion.
  • Listings and messages: retained while your account exists. Deleted on account deletion.
  • Identity documents: retained until your account is deleted, or upon your written request.
  • Push tokens: refreshed automatically; removed on account deletion.
  • Analytics events: anonymised; subject to PostHog's retention policy.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Access, correct, or update your profile information within the App (Settings screen)
  • Delete individual listings from your profile
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us (see Section 9)
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device's notification settings

We will respond to account deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored in plain text (managed by Supabase Auth). Identity documents are stored in a private, non-public storage bucket with row-level security. While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
